Facts About Virginia:

Virginia is one of the best states in the United States of America. People often underestimate Virginia, but they just need to learn more about it.
  1. Virginia is a Commonwealth- not technically a state
  2. Virginia's state motto is: Virginia is for Lovers
  3. Virginia is an essential part of American history for many reasons:
    • Virginia is one of the original 13 colonies
    • Eight U.S. Presidents are from Virginia (including three out of the first five Presidents)
    • The first permanent English settlement, Jamestown, is located in Virginia
  4. Virginia is the state with the most custom liscense plates, or vanity plates
  5. The first official Thanksgiving happened in Virginia
  6. Virginia has 22 National Parks

Virginia State Flag:

The Virginia State Flag is very unique. It features a woman dressed as an Amazon holding a spear and is standing on top of a fallen king. Below the Amazon, are the words "Sic semper tyrannis," which means "Thus always to tyrants" in Latin. This is symbolic of Virginia's role in helping during the American Revolution.
